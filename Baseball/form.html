Tryout Registration

Please Note: There is a $25 registration fee, this is a non refundable fee. Once this registration form is submitted please scroll down and add the Tryout Registration Fee to the shopping cart and make the payment. When the payment is received, the player will be added to the appropriate tryout date. There are a limited number of spots available for each tryout date. Please be advised that the player is added to the requested date only after the registration is paid. Thank you.
